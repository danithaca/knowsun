### Amazon Mechanical Turk R&D *(Ruby on Rails, Javascript)*
Amazon Mechanical Turk is a platform for "micro crowdsourcing" or "human computation" to accomplish tasks such as data labeling, content generation, usability studies, survey subjects recruitment, and more. We have many years of experience performing R&D using Amazon Mturk.
