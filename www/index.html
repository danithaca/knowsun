Amazon Mechanical Turk R&D
(Drupal, Ruby on Rails)

Amazon Mechanical Turk is a platform for "micro crowdsourcing" or "human computation" to accomplish tasks such as data labeling, content generation, usability study, survey subjects recruitment, etc. We have many years of experience doing R&D on Amazon Mturk.
